SEPTA Regional Rail Passenger Origins

by Casey C. Ross for the DVRPC

This application was created by Casey C. Ross at the University of Pennsylvania using data provided by the DVRPC. Driver origin data presented on this site is property of the DVRPC.

Using license plate survey data that is scrubbed of identifying information and aggregated into blocks, city and regional agencies can map the origins of those SEPTA passengers accessing regional rail by car. This analysis can help agencies understand station access mode share, and can help inform policy around infrastructure improvements near stations as well as parking availability and pricing.
